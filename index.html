MARIA SITU

I'm a product designer passionate about

creating exceptional experiences and telling good stories

in Austin, TX

Even as a child I knew I wanted to build worlds and tell stories. This path led me to pursue a film career in college where I spent many long days and nights working in different positions within the Art Department on film sets.

Some more memorable experiences include building a jail cell inside a rundown prison, walking alongside 8-feet tall magical birds looking for glowing eggs, and putting out bowls to collect water dripping from a lemon tree growing out of a man's stomach.

While in a different medium, this process of storytelling, of taking some intangible thing and making it real felt (and still feels) tremendously rewarding.

As designers, we are tasked with creating very specific experiences. We use our special set of problem solving skills to bridge the gaps left by user pain points and unaddressed needs.

I believe that design is a universal language and we use it as a tool to tell stories. It helps bring people together and allows us to share similar feelings and experiences that overcome physical or figurative barriers.

Want to chat? I'm interested in all things related to design, tech, art, and the human experience and how it all intersects. Connect with me!
