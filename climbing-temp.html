The Climbing Beta

a crowd-sourced review forum and business directory site for rock climbing.
Overview

Problem

Climbing is largely considered to be a friendly and social sport with great communities but users are having a hard time finding popular climbing routes and gyms while vacationing or in new areas.

Why is there no easy way to share knowledge about great outdoor climbs or indoor gyms?


Solution

Enter The Climbing Beta. The Climbing Beta gives users a solution for searching for climbing routes and gyms in areas they might not be familiar with.

Because the reviews and information given are 100% crowd-sourced and verified (when possible), users get the benefit of utilizing different local communities for honest and reliable reviews.
